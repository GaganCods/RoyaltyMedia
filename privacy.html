Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message content from contact forms
  • Donation information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To notify you about changes to our services
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our services
  • To monitor the usage of our services
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular backups
  • Employee training on data protection

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third-party analytics services
  • Payment processors for donations

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
